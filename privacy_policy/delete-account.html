Delete Your Mine Master Account

You can request the deletion of your Mine Master account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your username, email address, and password
  • Your country/profile information
  • All game scores, progress, and leaderboard entries
How to request deletion:

Send an email to manduulya@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your username and registered email address.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Any locally stored data on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.
